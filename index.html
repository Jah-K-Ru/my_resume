Mama Food Forest™: Lite Virtual Training
Northern Marianas College: Cooperative Research, Extension & Education Services; CNMI Green Growth Initiative
Program Date: November 2024-December 2024; Received: October 2024; Financial Sum: $00.00 (U.S. Currency)
Title: Cohort

Mama Food Forest™: Lite Virtual Training is a six-week virtual training that gives an overview of regenerative agroforestry. Participants will be exposed to the historical principles of regenerative agroforestry and its positive effects and threats of non-exposure throughout the indigenous population’s Pacific region. Throughout the virtual training, participants will be introduced to the technical notions of the program: logistical preparation, organic identification and usage, land survey and soil composition, climate/weather awareness, forestry design, and gardening applications. Near the end of the program, there will be a workshop to give the cohorts a hands-on experience on how to plant their food forests. By then, the cohorts will be recording the growth of their food forests throughout the weeks and months, and reporting it to the heads of the program.
